# Platform-Specific Rules

## Instagram
- **Primary Format**: Carousels (highest saves and shares)
- **Caption Style**: Hook → Value → CTA pattern
- **First Line**: THE most important — determines if people read more
- **Hashtags**: 20-30, mix of niche (#AItools, #LangChain) and broad (#tech, #AI)
- **Line Breaks**: Use liberally — wall of text = instant skip
- **Emoji**: 2-3 per post, used purposefully
- **Stories**: Promote post, polls, behind-the-scenes

## LinkedIn
- **Primary Format**: Thought leadership text + single image
- **Caption Style**: Hook in first 2 lines → insight → data → opinion → CTA
- **First 2 Lines**: Critical — appears before "see more" fold
- **Hashtags**: 3-5 only, professional and broad
- **Tone Shift**: More professional, data-driven, longer-form analysis ok
- **Engagement Hack**: Ask questions, make bold claims, use "Agree or disagree?"

## X (Twitter)
- **Primary Format**: Punchy single tweets, threads for deep-dives
- **Caption Style**: One sharp thought per tweet
- **280 Chars**: Every word must earn its place
- **Hashtags**: 1-3 max, often none
- **Tone Shift**: Most casual, most opinionated, most conversational
- **Hot Takes Welcome**: Contrarian views perform well

## Threads
- **Primary Format**: Short-form text, conversational
- **Caption Style**: Like talking to a smart friend at a coffee shop
- **Character Limit**: 500 chars
- **Hashtags**: 0-3, often none
- **Tone Shift**: Most authentic, less polished, more personal
- **Community Feel**: Reply-bait, "what's your experience?"
