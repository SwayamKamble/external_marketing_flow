# Content Pillars

## Pillar 1: AI News & Analysis
- **What**: Breaking AI news, product launches, model releases, industry moves
- **Angle**: Not just reporting — add analysis, hot takes, "what this means for you"
- **Formats**: News posts, single images, thread summaries
- **Frequency**: 1-2x per week
- **Example Topics**: "Claude 4 just dropped", "OpenAI + Apple deal", "EU AI Act impact"

## Pillar 2: Tutorials & How-To
- **What**: Step-by-step guides, tool walkthroughs, practical implementations
- **Angle**: Actionable, "you can do this right now" approach
- **Formats**: Carousels (best for step-by-step), reels
- **Frequency**: 2x per week
- **Example Topics**: "5 ways to use Claude for code review", "Build an AI chatbot in 30 min"

## Pillar 3: Tools & Resources
- **What**: Tool comparisons, workflow setups, resource roundups
- **Angle**: Curated recommendations with honest pros/cons
- **Formats**: Carousels, single images
- **Frequency**: 1-2x per week
- **Example Topics**: "Top 5 AI coding assistants", "My AI productivity stack"

## Pillar 4: Opinions & Debates
- **What**: Hot takes, industry debates, contrarian views
- **Angle**: Strong opinions backed by reasoning, inviting discussion
- **Formats**: Single images, reels
- **Frequency**: 1x per week
- **Example Topics**: "AGI is closer than you think", "Why prompt engineering is not dead"

## Pillar 5: Behind the Scenes
- **What**: Personal journey, building in public, lessons learned
- **Angle**: Authentic, relatable, vulnerability + insight
- **Formats**: Reels, single images
- **Frequency**: 1x per week (usually weekend)
- **Example Topics**: "What I learned building an AI startup", "My biggest AI mistake"

## Content Mix Rules
- Never have 2 consecutive days with the same pillar
- Ensure at least 1 carousel and 1 reel per week
- News posts only when genuinely newsworthy (not forced)
- Balance educational (savable) and engagement (shareable) content
